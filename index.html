About Me

Hello! My name is Nathaniel. I am a Colorado based full stack web developer. I am trained in the MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, and Node). I also have experience with creating front end designs using Bootstrap, JQuery, HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

I learned to code at the University of Denver and I have also had some experience working with marketing client's tracking environments (Google Adwords, Facebook Marketing and pixels, and Google Analytics) . These experiences have given me both technical proficiency as a developer and an understanding of the importance of team work and a great work culture.

I want to make software with great people and help small business grow to see their dreams come true. Please take a moment to look over my portfolio for examples of my work.
